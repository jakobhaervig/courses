About these slides

Press a to start the slide presentation with voice-over (make sure to have sound enabled).

  • Working principle of the slide format:
    • Use arrows (←, ↑, →, and ↓) to navigate the 2D slides.
      • → switches to next main section
      • ↓ digs deeper into topic with more details
    • Press "a" to stop/start the voice-over.
    • You may also just lean back, eat popcorn and let the slides auto-play. Should you miss something, you can easily stop, navigate back (press ← or ↑) and re-listen to the slide.
  • These slides are created based on the philosophy that:
    • The slides should be resonably standalone. I.e., they should work for both "live" traditional learning and "asynchronously" remote learning.
    • The slides should be easily accessible and shouldn't rely on anything else than a simple web browser.
    • The slides should be under version control and editable by approved teacher assistants (i.e. platform-independent and make use of open-source tools only).
  • Slide technicalities:
    • Slides are based on the reveal.js framework.
    • Source code is freely available at https://github.com/jakobhaervig/courses/heat-transfer.
    • A .pdf file of the latest slides is available at https://haervig.com/courses/heat-transfer/?print-pdf.
