Boundary condition types

The four boundary condition types

  • We have to specify boundary conditions on each non-internal node. Four types exist:

    • 1. Constant temperature

        • $T_0 = c$

    • 2. Constant heat flux

        • $\dot{Q}_\text{left,surface}=kA\frac{\text{d}T}{\text{d}x}=c$

    • 3. Convection

        • $\dot{Q}_\text{left,surface}=hA\left(T_\infty-T_0 \right)=c$

    • 4. Radiation

        • $\dot{Q}_\text{left,surface}=\epsilon \sigma A \left(T_\text{surr}^4-T_0^4\right)=c$

Incorporating the different boundary conditions

  • For BC type 1 (constant temperature), we simply fix the temperature
  • For BC type 2-4, we set up an energy balance on the volume element: $$ \small \begin{aligned} \small \sum_\text{all surfaces} \dot{Q} + \dot{G}_\text{element} = 0, \end{aligned} $$
    • which, in the case of a 1D plane wall, expands to

      • $$\small \dot{Q}_\text{left,surface} + kA \frac{T_1-T_0}{\Delta x}+\dot{g}_0\left(\frac{\Delta x}{2}A\right)=0.$$

  • Expression for $\dot{Q}_\text{left,surface}$ depends on BC type

Incorporating constant heat flux BC

  • A constant heat flux given by $\dot{q}$:

    • $$\small \dot{Q}_\text{left,surface} = \dot{q}_0 A$$

  • Energy balance at node $m=0$ then becomes:

    • $$\small \dot{q}_0 A + k A \frac{T_1-T_0}{\Delta x} + \dot{g}_0\left(\frac{\Delta x}{2}A\right) = 0$$

  • Special case of insulated boundary (zero heat flux):

    • $$\small 0 + k A \frac{T_1-T_0}{\Delta x} + \dot{g}_0\left(\frac{\Delta x}{2}A\right) = 0$$

Incorporating convection BC

  • Convection with heat transfer coefficient $h$:

    • $$\small \dot{Q}_\text{left,surface} = h A \left(T_\infty-T_0\right)$$

  • Energy balance at node $m=0$ then becomes:

    • $$\small h A \left(T_\infty-T_0\right) + k A \frac{T_1-T_0}{\Delta x} + \dot{g}_0\left(\frac{\Delta x}{2}A\right) = 0$$

Incorporating radiation BC

  • Radiation with emissivity $\epsilon$ and Stefan-Boltzmann constant $\sigma$:

    • $$\small \dot{Q}_\text{left,surface} = \epsilon \sigma A \left(T_\text{surr}^4-T_0^4\right)$$

  • Energy balance at node $m=0$ then becomes:

    • $$\small \epsilon \sigma A \left(T_\text{surr}^4-T_0^4\right) + k A \frac{T_1-T_0}{\Delta x} + \dot{g}_0\left(\frac{\Delta x}{2}A\right) = 0$$

  • Note that: For radiation the term $\small T_0^4$ makes the equation set non-linear. Different solution method is therefore typically needed.

Incorporating mixed boundary condition types

  • All BC types (heat flux, convection and radiation) can easily be added to form mixed types:

    • $$\small \dot{Q}_\text{left,surface}=$$

    $$\small~\dot{q}_0 A$$

    $$~+$$

    $$\small~h A \left(T_\infty-T_0\right)$$

    $$~+$$

    $$\small~\epsilon \sigma A \left(T_\text{surr}^4-T_0^4\right)$$
