Topic: Transient heat transfer with the energy balance method

Notation for transient problems

  • On space axis $m,n,k$ denote node number in $x,y,z$-directions
    • Distance between nodes is $\Delta x$
  • On time axis $i$ denote node number in $t$-direction (time)
    • Time between nodes is $\Delta t$
  • General notation:

    • $$ T_{m,n,k}^i $$

The energy balance equation for transient problems

  • We sum up heat transfer to/from volume element:

    $$ \small \left( \begin{array}{ccc} \text{Sum of heat}\\ \text{transfer rate}\\ \text{across surfaces}\\ \text{of volume element} \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{ccc} \text{Rate of heat}\\ \text{generation}\\ \text{inside the}\\ \text{element} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{ccc} \text{Rate of change}\\ \text{of the energy}\\ \text{content of}\\ \text{the element} \end{array} \right) $$

  • Change in energy content may be written as:

    • $$ \small \sum_\text{all surfaces} Q + \dot{G}_\text{element} = \frac{\Delta E_\text{element}}{\Delta t} $$

    $$ ~= \frac{m c_p \Delta T}{\Delta t} $$

    $$ ~= \frac{\rho V c_p \Delta T}{\Delta t} $$

    $$ ~= \frac{\rho V c_p (T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i)}{\Delta t} $$

Explicit and implicit solution methods

  • Explicit method:

    • $$ \small \sum_\text{all surfaces} Q^i + \dot{G}_\text{element}^i $$

    $$ ~= \frac{\rho V c_p (T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i)}{\Delta t} $$

  • Implicit method:

    • $$ \small \sum_\text{all surfaces} Q^{i+1} + \dot{G}_\text{element}^{i+1} $$

    $$ ~= \frac{\rho V c_p (T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i)}{\Delta t} $$

Interior nodes using explicit solution method

  • Explicit method:
    • $$\begin{aligned} kA\frac{\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m-1}^i}-\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m}^i}}{\Delta x} + kA\frac{\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m+1}^i}-\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m}^i}}{\Delta x}+ \dot{g}_\text{m}^iA\Delta x=\frac{\rho V c_p (T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i)}{\Delta t} \end{aligned}$$
  • Re-arranging:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#008000}{T_{m-1}^i}-2\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m}^i}+\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m+1}^i} + \frac{\dot{q}_m^i \Delta x^2}{k} = \frac{\Delta x^2}{\alpha \Delta t}\left(T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i\right) \end{aligned}$$
  • Defining $\tau=\alpha\Delta t/\Delta x^2$:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#008000}{T_{m-1}^i}-2\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m}^i}+\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m+1}^i} + \frac{\dot{q}_m^i \Delta x^2}{k} = \frac{1}{\tau}\left(T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i\right) \end{aligned}$$
  • Isolating the only unknown $\textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}}$:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}} = \tau(T_{m-1}^i+T_{m+1}^i)+(1-2\tau)T_m^i+\tau\frac{\dot{g}_m^i \Delta x^2}{k} \end{aligned}$$
  • 1 equation for each node with 1 unknown 😁

Boundary nodes using explicit solution method

  • Explicit method:
    • $$\begin{aligned} hA(T_\infty-\textcolor{#008000}{T_0^i}) + kA\frac{\textcolor{#008000}{T_1^i}-\textcolor{#008000}{T_0^i}}{\Delta x}+ \dot{g}_\text{m}^iA\frac{\Delta x}{2}=\frac{\rho V c_p (T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i)}{\Delta t} \end{aligned}$$
  • Re-arranging:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \left(\frac{-2h\Delta x}{k}-2\right)T_0^i+2T_{m+1}^i+\left(\frac{-2h\Delta x}{k}\right)T_\infty+\dot{g}_0^i\frac{(\Delta x)^2}{k} = \frac{\Delta x^2}{\alpha \Delta t}\left(T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i\right) \end{aligned}$$
  • Defining $\tau=\alpha\Delta t/\Delta x^2$:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#008000}{T_{m-1}^i}-2\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m}^i}+\textcolor{#008000}{T_{m+1}^i} + \frac{\dot{q}_m^i \Delta x^2}{k} = \frac{1}{\tau}\left(T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i\right) \end{aligned}$$
  • Isolating the only unknown $\textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}}$:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}} = \tau(T_{m-1}^i+T_{m+1}^i)+(1-2\tau)T_m^i+\tau\frac{\dot{g}_m^i \Delta x^2}{k} \end{aligned}$$
  • 1 equation for each node with 1 unknown 😁

Solution strategy for advancing in time with explicit solution methods

  • Step 1: Start at $t=0$ s with a known temperature field (initial condition)
  • Step 2: Solve eq to advance in time:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}} = \tau(T_{m-1}^i+T_{m+1}^i)+(1-2\tau)T_m^i+\tau\frac{\dot{g}_m^i \Delta x^2}{k} \end{aligned}$$
  • 1 equation for each node with 1 unknown 😁

Interior nodes using implicit solution methods

  • Implicit method:
    • $$\begin{aligned} kA\frac{\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m-1}^{i+1}}-\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m}^{i+1}}}{\Delta x} + kA\frac{\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m+1}^{i+1}}-\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m}^{i+1}}}{\Delta x}+ \dot{G}_\text{element}^{i+1}=\frac{\rho V c_p (T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i)}{\Delta t} \end{aligned}$$
  • Re-arranging:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m-1}^{i+1}}-2\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m}^{i+1}}+\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m+1}^{i+1}} + \frac{\dot{q}_m^{i+1} \Delta x^2}{k} = \frac{\Delta x^2}{\alpha \Delta t}\left(T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i\right) \end{aligned}$$
  • Using $\tau=\alpha\Delta t/\Delta x^2$:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m-1}^{i+1}}-2\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m}^{i+1}}+\textcolor{#524fa1}{T_{m+1}^{i+1}} + \frac{1}{\tau} = \frac{\Delta x^2}{\alpha \Delta t}\left(T_m^{i+1}-T_m^i\right) \end{aligned}$$
  • Seperating the 3 unknowns $\textcolor{#b52025}{T_{m-1}^{i+1}}$, $\textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}}$ and $\textcolor{#b52025}{T_{m+1}^{i+1}}$:
    • $$\begin{aligned} \tau\textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}} - (1+2\tau) \textcolor{#b52025}{T_m^{i+1}} + \tau \textcolor{#b52025}{T_{m+1}^{i+1}} + \tau \frac{\dot{g}_m^{i+1}\Delta x^2}{k} + T_m^i = 0 \end{aligned}$$
  • Set of $M$ equations with each 3 unknown 😒
