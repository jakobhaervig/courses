Topic: Semi-infinite solids

What are semi-infinite solids?

  • An ideal body with a single plane surface (extends to infinity in all directions)
    • Fast to compute, e.g. doing millions of evaluations
    • Yield exact results, e.g. excellent for verifying other numerical codes
  • The bad
    • Limited to simplified geometries:
      • Infinitely long plane wall
      • Infinitely long cylinderl
      • Perfect sphere
    • Limited to simple conditions only:
      • Uniform and temperature-independent properties
      • No heat generation inside, radiation on boundary etc
      • Uniform initial temperature
      • Only convection on boundary with constant coefficient
