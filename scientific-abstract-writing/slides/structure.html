Course in Scientific abstract writing

Topic: Structure

The abstract structure

  • Basic introduction: Basic introduction to the field; accessible to scientists of any discipline (1 sentence!)
  • More detailed background: More details on the background; accessible to scientists within related disciplines (2-3 sentences!)
  • Problem being adressed: Clear statement of the problem being adressed in the present study (1 sentences!)
  • Main result: Clear statement of the problem being adressed in the present study (1 sentence!)
  • Comparison to previous studies/status quo: Elaborating on main result and/or comparison with previouds studies (2-3 sentences!)
  • General context: Putting the results in a broarder context (1-2 sentences!)
  • Broader perspective: Summarise main findings in a way accessible to scientists in any discipline (2-3 sentences!)

Approx 10-14 sentences (approx 200-300 words)
