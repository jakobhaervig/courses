Course in Scientific abstract writing

Topic: Two types of abstracts

Type 1: Conference acceptance abstract

  • Main purpose: Get accepted to present at conference
  • Deadline for submission: Couple of months (typically 3 to 6) before conference (see conference website for exact date)
  • Important point: Results are most likely not available when you submit this! Write what you expect your paper will be about

Type 2: Abstract as part of a scientific paper

  • Main purpose: Outline the content of the paper
  • Scope: Get accepted for presenting at conference
  • Deadline for submission: Included as part of the scientific paper (see conference website for exact date)
  • Important point: All information is available at this point and it should therefore be updated/rewritten
